## Overview

Soul Blueprint brings five timeless systems — Western astrology, Human Design, the Gene Keys, the I Ching, and Vedic (Jyotish) — into a single, coherent reading of a person's life. Users answer a few questions, see a free preview in minutes, and can unlock a full 35+ page personalized report for a one-time payment (no subscription).

The point isn't prediction — it's awareness: surfacing the patterns someone is prone to repeating so they can work with them consciously.

## My Role

A founder project under SagaVortex, owned end-to-end — engineering, product, design and go-to-market.

**Key Responsibilities:**

- Product engineering across the full stack (Next.js, TypeScript, React)
- Astrology/astronomy engine driven by a professional-grade ephemeris (exact planetary positions for a given time and place of birth)
- Multi-step questionnaire and free-preview flow, then a paid full report
- Report generation and keepsake-quality PDF export (chart wheel, bodygraph, 35+ pages)
- One-time payments and secure checkout
- Branding, UI/UX, long-form copywriting, SEO and deployment

## Technical Highlights

- **Real astronomy, not templates**: every placement is calculated from an ephemeris for the exact birth moment, then synthesised across all five systems
- **Report engine**: turns raw chart data into readable, personalized long-form content and a downloadable PDF
- **Commerce**: free preview → one-time unlock (€22.20), privacy-first (no data resale)
- **Polished front-end**: calm, editorial design with a cosmic aesthetic and responsive layouts

## Why It Matters

Soul Blueprint is a complete, self-shipped product — engine, content, commerce, and design — demonstrating the full arc of taking an idea from concept to a live, paid offering.
