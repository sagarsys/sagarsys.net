## Overview

SoulPrints is the fine-art print storefront for SagaVortex Art Photography — an editorial, image-led experience ("Moments, made to keep") for browsing and buying museum-quality prints, based in Amsterdam.

## My Role

A founder project under SagaVortex. I own the design and front-end end-to-end — art direction, editorial layout, motion, and the storefront/buying experience.

## Highlights

- **Editorial, image-first design** with a calm, premium aesthetic that puts the photography front and centre
- **Motion & interaction** — scroll-driven reveals and refined micro-interactions
- **Commerce** — a clean path from browsing to purchasing fine-art prints
- **Brand-consistent** with the wider SagaVortex identity
