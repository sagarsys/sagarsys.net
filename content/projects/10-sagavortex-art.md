## Overview

The main website for SagaVortex Art Photography — an Amsterdam-based landscape photographer and fine-art storyteller. A dark, immersive, image-led experience ("Immerse yourself in the Visual Vortex of Nature") spanning galleries, prints, events and journey.

## My Role

A founder project — I own the brand identity and the full site design: art direction, information architecture, layout, typography and content. Implemented on Squarespace so the business can update it independently.

## Highlights

- **Brand identity & art direction** for the SagaVortex name
- **Editorial, photography-first UI/UX** with a dark, immersive aesthetic
- **Full IA** across Gallery, Prints, Events, Journey and About
- Built on **Squarespace** for maintainability by a non-technical operator

_Included as a design / brand piece — the engineering work lives in the other projects._
